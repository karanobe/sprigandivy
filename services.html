F.A.Qs

What will working with a wedding planner entail?

Depending upon which package you choose, your wedding planner is there to organize and make sense of all the moving parts when planning a wedding. They will assist you with creating a budget, selecting vendors, creating a timeline for the day and the overall seamless execution of your big day!

Why do I need a day of coordinator?

Weddings are expensive, but the one vendor that is crucial to making your big day a worry-free success is your coordinator! The day of coordinator plays the integral role of managing your vendors, following a timeline for the day, and executing all the fine details to make your vision come alive.

Do you offer custom wedding packages if the three wedding options don’t apply to me?

Absolutely! The three packages we offer may not suit your needs or you may have specific events that span a few days. Whatever the case may be, we are more than happy to customize a package that suits your specific event needs.
